The Situation of Sitio Bebe

Sitio Bebe is a town where there is a shortage of drinking water, because the town is not near
any natural sources of water. Even though balon’s or water are open, this still makes drinkable water
scarce, and that they have to boil the water before drinking it.

Based on the table, the percentage of cases in Sitio Bebe of (acute respiratory infection(59%),
acute lower respiratory tract infection(6%), and hypertension(5.5%)), are higher than the cases
of Central Luzon which even has a larger population than Sitio Bebe, cases of common diseases
of Sitio Bebe in Central Luzon: acute respiratory infection(48.%), acute lower respiratory tract
infection(6% ), and hypertension(5.5%). One of the reasons for having common diseases in Sitio
Bebe is that usually work in the town is mining or factory work. In mining, the diseases can be
caused by the coal or other 'ores' that they inhale that harms their respiratory system, it can
also be the factory because of the smoke it emits from the engine, and that you are in an
enclosed area or building. And if a person gets one of the diseases, the process of consulting
with a doctor would be slow, sure there are training centers but there aren’t even colleges that
would have health professional courses, even the colleges are from other cities, this would lead
to short staff in hospitals or clinics

Our Solution

Our Group decided to create a fundraising concert to gain money to help the residents of Sitio Bebe, and informed our audience via facebook, or youtube through an educational video.

Who are the people that we need?

School heads
25% of the profited funds from the concert would fund colleges that will support health professional courses.

The Residents of Sitio Bebe
So that they can be aware of the diseases that they could acquire, 50% of the profit would go to the barangay to assist them, and 25% of the profit would go to clean water providers so that they wouldn’t need to boil the water from water pumps.

The Youths
The concert would help letting other people know especially the young generation the situation of Sitio Bebe.
